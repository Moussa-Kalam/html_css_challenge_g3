YOUR RECIPE

Ingredients

  1. 2 pounds skinless chicken legs
  2. Lemon juice
  3. 2 large red onions, finely chopped
  4. 1 1/2 berbere (Ethiopian spice mixture which includes chile pepper, garlic, ginger, dried basil, Ehiopian cardamom, black and white pepper, fenugreeek and rue, a bitter shrub)
  5. 1 cup Niter Kibbeh (Ethiopian Spiced Butter), recipe follows:
    • 6 pounds unsalted butter
    • 1/3 cup bishop seed (also known as ajwain, similar to thyme)
    • 1/3 cup cardamom seeds
    • 1/3 cup black cumin
    • 1/3 cup koseret (dried woodsy flavored herb, dried oregano can be substituted)
  6. 1/2 cup fresh garlic, finely ground
  7. 1/2 cup fresh ginger, finely ground
  8. 6 peeled hard-boiled eggs
  9. Salt
  10. Injera (Fermented Sourdough Bread, for serving, recipe follows:
    • 1 1/2 pounds teff flour
    • 1/2 pound barley flour
    • 1/4 cup wheat flour
